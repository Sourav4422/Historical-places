Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra built by Shah Jahan for Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort

A historic fort in Delhi that was the residence of Mughal emperors for 200 years.

Qutub Minar

The world's tallest brick minaret, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi.

Gateway of India

A triumphal arch in Mumbai built during the British rule in India.

Hawa Mahal

A palace in Jaipur known for its unique architecture and "honeycomb" windows.

Mysore Palace

A stunning palace in Mysore, known for its royal architecture and history.

Ajanta Caves

Rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments in Maharashtra dating from the 2nd century BC.

Charminar

A mosque and monument in Hyderabad, an iconic symbol of the city.

India Gate

A war memorial located in New Delhi, commemorating soldiers of the British Indian Army.

Meenakshi Temple

A historic Hindu temple located in Madurai, dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi.

Sun Temple

A 13th-century temple in Konark, Odisha, dedicated to the Hindu Sun God, Surya.

Victoria Memorial

A large marble building in Kolkata, built to honor Queen Victoria.
